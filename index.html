About Us

Deborah Lyon is a certified Lymphoedema therapist and massage specialist. She has always been drawn to the philosophy of helping others,

"Helping one person might not change the world, but it could change the world for one person"

With over 20 years experience, Deborah now specialises in the lymphatic system, with extensive knowledge, dealing with complex cases and compromised lymphatic systems.

Qualified in FG-MLD (fluoroscopy guided manual lymphatic drainage) LTA cert, and as a member of MLD UK and the British Lymphology Society, you can be assured you're in safe hands.

Working in Oncology, Deborah quickly realised a more in depth knowledge was needed of the lymphatic system, which also led her onto a journey discovering scar therapy.

Trained with Restore Scar Therapy Deborah uses evidence-informed techniques to help improve scar tissue and release adhesions, giving advice to clients on how to self manage their own healing.

Incorporating her extensive knowledge of lymphatic drainage, massage and scar therapy, Deborah is able to help many different clients recover post-operatively. Her knowledge and techniques help to minimise pain and inflammation, recovering faster and enhancing your surgical results.

Her techniques are beneficial for both reconstructive or plastic surgeries or procedures such as liposuction for lipoedema.

Deborah also provides lymphatic treatments to sculpt and define your body with visible results after just one session, reducing swelling, bloating and fluid retention, improving skin texture, cellulite and boosting the metabolism.

The Brazilian lymphatic sculpting treatment is very popular with clients wanting to feel more sculpted and energised.

Deborah's clients love her knowledge and ability to turn her hand using different techniques to achieve the best results possible, making sure each treatment is tailor made to each individual client.
